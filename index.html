About me

A little bit about me and what i have been doing these last few years.

  • 2012

    Graduation in industrial sciences

    In highschool I studied industrial sciences at Edugo Glorieux in Oostakker (Belgium). This is a technical education with a lot of maths and science courses such as physics and chemistry and technical courses such as mechanics, electronics and electricity.

  • 2012 - 2016

    Bachelors degree in Electronics-ICT

    I obtained my first bachelors degree in ICT at Odisee University College in Gent (Belgium). This is a general ICT education containing web development, programming in C# and Java, networking and system administration.

  • 2016

    Internship at Cartamundi Digital

    The final semester of my first degree was an internship. I did my Internship at Cartamundi Digital where i worked on a mobile card game made with the Unity Engine and a few other projects using C# and WPF.

  • 2016 - Present

    Bachelors degree in Game Development, Digital Arts and Entertainment

    After my first degree I knew i wanted to do more with programming. To combine this with a passion for videogames I enrolled in Digital Arts and Entertainment for a second degree, one that focusses on programming. Currently i am in the third and last year for this degree.

  • 2018

    Erasmus Study at Abertay University, Dundee

    For my final year in Digital Arts and Entertainment I am spending the first semester on erasmus in Scotland. I see this as a great opportunity to learn more about subjects not covered in DAE and broaden my skills in general.
