Outta Da Way

A chaotic sidescrolling PVP endless runner

This is a project I made together with four other students. It is a offline multiplayer game where the goal is to survive as long as possible while trying to defeat your opponents. The various traps and powerups scattered through the procedurally generated level make for a fun and chaotic experience where two games are never truly the same.



What did I do? The main thing I worked on was the procedural generation of the level and the background. I made sure that the level was created a little bit ahead of the camera and deleted a little bit behind the camera, all while making sure the level made sense. for example making sure the highest level only starts when it is possible to reach it and spawning falling spikes straps only when the level above is active, so they are not floating in the air. The frequency whith which the traps and powerups spawn can be configured in the menu if you really like (or dislike) certain items.

In order to play the game as intended you will need at least 2 controllers (maximum 4), but to get an idea of the game you can “play” it solo as well.
